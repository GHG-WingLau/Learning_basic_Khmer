The 5-Minute Street Evangelism Flow

5分鐘街頭福音對話流程

Since street encounters are transient, you must move quickly and respectfully. This script is designed to establish relationship, address objections, share Jesus, and pray in under five minutes.
由於街頭遇見的時間短暫，對話必須快速且充滿尊重。本流程設計用於在5分鐘內建立關係、解答疑慮、分享耶穌，並帶入禱告。

1

Step 1: Relate & Care (Minutes 0-1)

步驟 1: 建立關係與關懷 (0-1 分鐘)

Greet politely. Note their context (e.g., street vendor is hot, driver is tired). Express genuine care to build a bridge.
有禮貌地問候。注意他們的處境（例如：路邊攤販很熱、司機很累）。表達真誠的關心，建立互信橋樑。

Example Greeting / 範例問候:

ជំរាបសួរ អ៊ំ! ខ្ញុំស្រឡាញ់ប្រទេសខ្មែរ! តើអ៊ំសុខសប្បាយទេ?

"Choum-reap-sour Om! Khnhom srolanh bray-tes khmer! Tae om sok-saby te?"

"Hello Uncle! I love Cambodia! Are you doing well?"

「叔叔您好！我很喜愛柬埔寨！您最近過得好嗎？」

2

Step 2: Locate the Spiritual Need (Minutes 1-2)

步驟 2: 引導至屬靈需求 (1-2 分鐘)

Transition into spiritual topics by offering immediate value. Ask if you can share a story of a Father's love, or ask about their daily burden.
透過提供屬靈慰藉切入話題。詢問是否能分享關於天父慈愛的故事，或關心他們日常的重擔。

Bridge Statement / 橋樑對話:

ខ្ញុំមានដំណឹងល្អពីព្រះជាម្ចាស់ ដែលអាចផ្តល់សេចក្តីសុខសម្រាប់អ៊ំ។

"Khnhom mean dom-neung-laor pi Preah-cheah-mchas dae arch phdor-ni-sokh som-rap om."

"I have Good News from the Creator God that can bring peace to you."

「我有一個來自創造主上帝的好消息，能為您帶來平安。」

3

Step 3: Proclaim the Finished Work (Minutes 2-4)

步驟 3: 宣講耶穌成就的救贖 (2-4 分鐘)

Explain the Gospel: Creator Father God loves us but sin separates us. We try to make merit (Karmic cycle) but cannot wash sin away. Jesus is the Son of God, took our bad karma (sin), died, and rose again!
傳講福音：創造主天父愛我們，但罪惡使我們與祂分離。人類試圖積德行善（因果輪迴）卻無法洗淨罪。耶穌是上帝的獨生子，承擔了我們所有的惡業（罪），受死並復活！

Core Gospel Summary / 福音精要:

ព្រះយេស៊ូវបានសុគតដើម្បីលុបលាងបាបទាំងអស់របស់ខ្ញុំ និងអ៊ំ។ រួចព្រះអង្គមានជន្មរស់ឡើងវិញ!

"Preah-ye-sov ban sok-kot deim-bey lop-leang bab-rop-os khnhom neng om. Ruoch Preah-ong mean chon roas laung vinh!"

"Jesus died to cleanse away your sins and my sins. Then He rose back to life!"

「耶穌受死是為了洗淨您和我的罪孽，然後祂復活了！」

4

Step 4: The Prayer Invitation (Minute 4-5)

步驟 4: 邀請一同禱告 (4-5 分鐘)

Ask for permission to pray over them *right now*. In Khmer culture, praying for blessings, health, and peace is highly welcome, even among devout Buddhists. This is when the Spirit works!
請求允許*現在*為他們祈禱。在柬埔寨文化中，為祝福、健康和平安祈禱是非常受歡迎的，即使對虔誠的佛教徒也是如此。這是聖靈動工的時刻！

Prayer Ask / 邀請禱告文:

តើខ្ញុំអាចអធិស្ឋានសម្រាប់អ៊ំឥឡូវនេះ ដើម្បីឲ្យព្រះផ្តល់សេចក្តីសុខសប្បាយ និងល្អបានទេ?

"Tae khnhom arch a-thi-sathan som-rap om deim-bey aoy Preah phdor sok-saby neng laor ban te?"

"Can I pray for you right now so God will give you peace and goodness?"

「我現在可以為您禱告，讓上帝賜予您平安與美好嗎？」

Key Cultural Tips

柬埔寨宣教核心文化指南

  • ✓
    Body Language (Sampeah) / 合十禮: Join your palms together at your chest when greeting or saying thank you. It shows immense respect.
    打招呼或道謝時，雙手合十置於胸前。這展現了極大的敬意。
  • ✓
    The Karma Trap / 因果輪迴的框架: Buddhists believe in doing good to cancel bad. Emphasize that Jesus paid the karma price that human effort can't wash away.
    佛教徒相信靠行善彌補業障。強調耶穌已經代替我們付清了人類靠自己無法洗淨的罪障（業障）代價。
  • ✓
    Don't Argue / 切勿辯論: If they say they cannot change ancestors' religion, respond gently with: "I respect your ancestors. Jesus command us to honor parents."
    如果他們表明不能背棄祖先的信仰，溫和地回應：「我非常尊重您的祖先。耶穌也命令我們孝敬父母。」

Why pray immediately?

為什麼要立即為他們禱告？

Intellectual arguments rarely win the hearts of locals. However, a compassionate, bold prayer in the name of **Jesus (Preah Ye-sov)** breaks spiritual chains.

理性的辯論很難贏得當地人的心。然而，奉**耶穌 (Preah Ye-sov)** 之名進行充滿慈愛、大有能力的禱告，能夠打破屬靈的鎖鏈，親自體會上帝的真實。
