Luke 15:11-32 Stage Play

The Grace of the Father

A 15-Minute, 3-Act Stage Play specifically designed for multilingual amateur actors, focusing on the boundless mercy and grace of the Father.

Cast: Grace (Narrator) • Wing (Father) • Issac (Elder Son) • Lun (Younger Son) • Yan (Hog Owner) • Various Players
Character Focus / Practice Mode:
Visible Languages:

Staging Setup & Production Notes / 舞台佈置與製作說明

Props Suggested / 建議道具：

  • Act I / 第一幕： A leather pouch/bag representing money inheritance. (代表財產繼承的皮革錢包/袋子。)
  • Act II / 第二幕： Mud/straw on floor, a wooden bowl with pig pods. (地板上的泥土/稻草，裝有豬豆莢的木碗。)
  • Act III / 第三幕： A premium bright colored robe, a gold ring, sandals. (一件高級鮮豔的袍子、一枚金戒指、涼鞋。)

Acting Guidelines (Amateurs) / 演員表演指引（業餘演員適用）：

  • Keep expressions big! Stage acting relies on physical body language. (表情要誇張！舞台表演非常依賴肢體語言。)
  • Wing (Father) should show physical tenderness: open arms, warm smile. (父親 Wing 應展現身體上的溫柔：張開雙臂，帶著溫暖的微笑。)
  • Take pauses between languages to let the crowd digest the emotional scenes. (在不同語言的台詞之間稍作停頓，讓觀眾消化情感戲劇。)

Act I: The Request & Departure

Scene 1

Stage Setup: Center stage has a comfortable bench representing the Father's wealthy home. Wing (Father) is sitting on the bench reading or checking scrolls. Issac (Elder Son) is working hard on the right, carrying tools or looking over ledgers. Lun (Younger Son) stands on the left side, looking restless and looking off-stage.

舞台佈置：舞台中央有一張舒適的長椅，代表父親富裕的家。父親（Wing）坐在長椅上閱讀或查看卷軸。哥哥（Issac）在右側辛勤工作，拿著工具或查看賬本。小兒子（Lun）站在左側，顯得焦躁不安並望向台外。

Grace (Narrator) Grace

Once, there was a loving father who had two sons. They had everything they needed in their home. Yet, the younger son wanted something more.

從前，有一位慈祥的父親，他有兩個兒子。他們在家中應有盡有，但小兒子卻渴望著別的東西。

កាលពីមុន មានឪពុកដ៏មានសេចក្ដីស្រឡាញ់ម្នាក់ មានកូនប្រុសពីរនាក់។ ពួកគេមានអ្វីៗគ្រប់យ៉ាងដែលត្រូវការនៅក្នុងផ្ទះ។ ប៉ុន្តែ កូនពៅចង់បានអ្វីដែលច្រើនជាងនេះ។

Kal pi mun, mean ov-puk dor mean sec-kdei sror-lanh mnak, mean koun proh pi neak. Puk-kei mean avei-avei krob-yang kdel trov-kar noat-knong ptheah. Bon-tae koun-pov chong-ban avei kdel chreun cheang nih.

Lun (Younger Son) Lun / [To Father, with confidence]

"Father, give me my share of the estate now. I want to build my own life."

「父親，請把我財產中我應得的一份現在就給我。我想過我自己的生活。」

«លោកឪពុក សូមប្រគល់ចំណែកមរដកដែលត្រូវចែកដល់ខ្ញុំមកឥឡូវនេះ។ ខ្ញុំចង់កសាងជីវិតផ្ទាល់ខ្លួនរបស់ខ្ញុំ។»

"Lok ov-puk, soum bro-kol chom-nek mor-dor kdel trov chek dol knhom mok el-lov-nih. Knhom chong kor-sang chi-vit phtol-khluon ro-bos knhom."

Wing (Father) Wing / [Looking saddened but gentle]

"My son, are you sure you want to leave? This home is always open to you."

「我的孩子，你確定要離開嗎？這裡永遠是你的家。」

«កូនប្រុសរបស់ពុក តើកូនពិតជាចង់ចាកចេញមែនទេ? ផ្ទះនេះតែងតែបើកស្វាគមន៍កូនជានិច្ច។»

"Koun proh ro-bos puk, tae koun pit-cheor chong chak-chenh men-te? Ptheah nih teng-tae berk sva-kom koun cheor-nich."

Lun (Younger Son) Lun / [Determined, turning away]

"Yes, Father. I want to see the world. I want my freedom."

「是的，父親。我想去看看世界，我想要我的自由。」

«បាទ លោកឪពុក។ ខ្ញុំចង់ទៅមើលពិភពលោក។ ខ្ញុំចង់បានសេរីភាព。»

"Bat lok ov-puk. Knhom chong tov merl pi-pob lok. Knhom chong ban se-rei-pheap."

Wing (Father) Wing / [Hands over the pouch of money, with deep emotion]

"(Sighs) If this is your wish, I will grant it. May peace be with you, my beloved son."

「（嘆息）如果這是你的心願，我便成全你。願你一路平安，我心愛的孩子。」

«（ដកដង្ហើមធំ）បើនេះជាបំណងរបស់កូន ពុកនឹងចែកឲ្យកូន。 សូមឲ្យកូនជួបតែសេចក្តីសុខសាន្ត កូនសម្លាញ់របស់ពុក។»

"(Dok-dong-herm thom) Ber nih cheor bom-nong ro-bos koun, puk neng chek oy koun. Soum oy koun chuob tae sec-kdei sok-sant, koun sror-lanh ro-bos puk."

Stage Action: Lun takes the pouch, smiles happily, waves, and walks off-stage with high energy. Wing watches him leave with a hand on his chest, visibly hurting. Issac walks up to Wing, looking unhappy with Lun's departure, but says nothing yet. Lights fade down slowly.

舞台動作：Lun 接過錢包，高興地微笑、揮手，並精力充沛地走下舞台。Wing 手撫胸口看著他離去，顯然十分痛心。Issac 走向 Wing，對 Lun 的離去顯得不高興，但暫時什麼也沒說。燈光漸漸變暗。

Act II: The Fall & Repentance

Scene 2

Stage Setup: The home bench is removed. Stage looks darker. On the right, some party-goers (Bystanders/Friends) are holding cups, laughing with Lun. Lun is throwing money around, acting drunk and arrogant. Soon, the scene shifts—the friends run away with his remaining money, leaving Lun alone, dirty and hungry.

舞台佈置：移開家中的長椅。舞台顯得較暗。在右側，一些派對參與者（旁觀者/朋友）拿著杯子，與 Lun 一起大笑。Lun 在到處扔錢，表現得醉醺醺且傲慢。很快，場景轉變——朋友們帶著他剩下的錢跑掉，留下 Lun 一個人，又髒又餓。

Grace (Narrator) Grace

The younger son traveled to a distant country. There, he wasted all his father's money on wild living. Soon, a terrible hunger struck the land, and he had nothing left.

小兒子去了遙遠的國家。在那裡，他生活放蕩，揮霍了父親給他的所有財產。不久，那地遭遇了嚴重的饑荒，他變得一無所有。

កូនពៅបានធ្វើដំណើរទៅឯស្រុកឆ្ងាយ។ នៅទីនោះ វាបានបង្ហិនទ្រព្យសម្បត្តិរបស់ឪពុកទាំងអស់ដោយល្បែងស៊ីផឹកហួសប្រមាណ។ មិនយូរប៉ុន្មាន កើតមានអំណត់ជាខ្លាំងនៅស្រុកនោះ ហើយវាក៏លែងមានអ្វីសល់ទៀតដែរ។

Koun pov ban thver dom-ner tov ek srok chngay. Noat ti-noh, vea ban bong-hen throp-som-bat ro-bos ov-puk tang-os doy l-beng si-phek huoh-bro-man. Men yu bon-man, kert mean om-not cheor khlang noat srok noh, heuy vea kor leng mean avei sol tiet der.

Friends (Various People) Various Friends / [Laughing mockingly, rejecting Lun]

"No money, Lun? Then you are no friend of ours! Get away from us!"

「沒錢了，阿倫（Lun）？那你就不是我們的朋友！離我們遠點！」

«គ្មានលុយទៀតទេមែនទេ លន់? បើអញ្ចឹងឯងមិនមែនជាមិត្តភក្តិរបស់យើងទៀតទេ! ទៅឱ្យឆ្ងាយពីយើងទៅ!»

"Kmean luy tiet te men-te, Lun? Ber-on-cheng eng men-men cheor mett-pheak ro-boh yerng tiet te! Tov oy chngay pi yerng tov!"

Yan (Hog Owner) Yan / [Harshly, pointing to the ground]

"You want a job? Fine. Go feed my pigs on the farm. That is the only work for you!"

「你想找工作？好吧。去我的農場替我餵豬。這是我唯一能給你的差事！」

«ឯងចង់បានការងារធ្វើមែនទេ? ល្អហើយ! ទៅឃ្វាលហ្វូងជ្រូករបស់ខ្ញុំនៅលើចំការទៅ។ នោះជាការងារតែមួយគត់សម្រាប់ឯង!»

"Eng chong ban kar-ngeor thver men-te? L-or heuy! Tov khveal fvong chrouk ro-boh knhom noat ler chom-kar tov. Noh cheor kar-ngeor tae mouy kot som-rab eng!"

Lun (Younger Son) Lun / [Kneeling, holding a pig-food bowl, crying]

"I am so hungry... Even this pig food looks delicious. Nobody will help me."

「我好餓... 就連這些餵豬的豆莢，看起來都那麼好吃。沒有任何人願意幫我。」

«ខ្ញុំឃ្លានខ្លាំងណាស់... សូម្បីតែចំណីជ្រូកនេះក៏គួរឱ្យចង់ស៊ីដែរ។ គ្មាននរណាម្នាក់ជួយខ្ញុំទាល់តែសោះ។»

"Knhom khlean khlang nah... soum-bi tae chom-nei chrouk nih kor kuor oy chong si der. Kmean nor-na mnak chuoy knhom thal-tae soh."

Lun (Younger Son) Lun / [Sudden realization, looking upward with hope]

"My father’s servants have plenty of food, but here I am, dying of hunger! I will go home to my father!"

「我父親有多少的雇工，口糧都有餘，我竟然在這裡餓死嗎！我要回我父親那裡去！」

«តើមានជើងឈ្នួលរបស់លោកឪពុកខ្ញុំប៉ុន្មាននាក់ ដែលមានអាហារបរិបូរ? តែនៅទីនេះ ខ្ញុំជិតដាច់ពោះស្លាប់ហើយ! ខ្ញុំនឹងត្រឡប់ទៅរកលោកឪពុកវិញ!»

"Tae mean cherng-chnuol ro-bos lok ov-puk knhom pon-man neak, kdel mean ar-har bo-ri-bour? Tae noat ti-nih, knhom chit dach-poh slab heuy! Knhom neng tror-lop tov rok lok ov-puk vinh!"

Lun (Younger Son) Lun / [Rehearsing his apology, crying with humility]

"I will say: 'Father, I have sinned against heaven and against you. Treat me as one of your servants.'"

「我要說：『父親！我得罪了天，也得罪了你。請把我當作你的一個雇工吧。』」

«ខ្ញុំនឹងនិយាយថា៖ លោកឪពុក ខ្ញុំបានធ្វើបាបនឹងព្រះ ហើយនឹងលោកឪពុកដែរ សូមទទួលខ្ញុំទុកដូចជាជើងឈ្នួលរបស់លោកឪពុកវិញចុះ។»

"Knhom neng ni-yeay tha: Lok ov-puk, knhom ban thver-bab neng prah, heuy neng lok ov-puk der, soum to-tuol knhom tok-doch-cheor cherng-chnuol ro-bos lok ov-puk vinh choh."

Stage Action: Lun slowly stands up, limping, looking weak, and begins walking slowly to the other side of the stage representing the journey back home. Lights adjust from blue/dim to a warm yellow.

舞台動作：Lun 慢慢站起來，一拐一拐地走著，顯得十分虛弱，並開始慢慢走向舞台的另一側，代表回家的旅程。燈光從藍色/昏暗調整為溫慢的黃色。

Act III: The Father's Grace & Celebration

Scene 3

Stage Setup: Center stage has the home bench restored. Wing (Father) stands, looking into the distance as if waiting. Suddenly he spots Lun at a distance. He throws away his walking stick and runs toward Lun.

舞台佈置：舞台中央恢復了家中的長椅。父親（Wing）站著，望向遠方，彷彿在等待。突然，他遠遠地看見了 Lun。他扔掉拐杖，奔向 Lun。

Grace (Narrator) Grace

So he got up and went to his father. But while he was still a long way off, his father saw him and was filled with compassion.

於是他站起來，往他父親那裡去。相離還遠，他父親看見，就動了慈心。

រួចវាក៏ក្រោកឡើងដើរទៅរកឪពុកវា។ លុះឪពុកឃើញកូនពីចម្ងាយហើយ ក៏មានចិត្តអាណិតអាសូរក្រៃលែង។

Ruoch vea kor krok-lerng der tov rok ov-puk vea. Luh ov-puk merl-khern koun pi chom-ngay heuy, kor mean chit ar-nit ar-sour krei-leng.

Wing (Father) Wing / [Running towards Lun with open arms, crying out]

"Lun! My son! Is that really you?!"

「阿倫（Lun）！我的孩子！真的是你嗎？！」

«លន់! កូនប្រុសរបស់ពុក! តើពិតជាកូនមែនទេ?!»

"Lun! Koun proh ro-bos puk! Tae pit-cheor cheor koun men-te?!"

Lun (Younger Son) Lun / [Kneeling in front of Father, crying]

"Father, I have sinned against heaven and you. I am not worthy to be your son!"

「父親！我得罪了天，也得罪了你。我再也不配稱為你的兒子！」

«លោកឪពុក ខ្ញុំបានធ្វើបាបនឹងព្រះ ហើយនឹងលោកឪពុកដែរ ខ្ញុំមិនគួរឱ្យគេហៅជាកូនរបស់លោកឪពុកទៀតទេ។»

"Lok ov-puk, knhom ban thver-bab neng prah, heuy neng lok ov-puk der, knhom men kuor oy kei hav cheor koun ro-bos lok ov-puk tiet te."

Wing (Father) Wing / [Pulls Lun up to a hug, wiping his tears, calling to servants]

"No, my son! (To servants) Bring the best robe! Put a ring on his finger and sandals on his feet!"

「不，我的孩子！（對僕人們說）快拿最好的袍子給他穿上！把戒指戴在他指頭上，把鞋穿在他腳上！」

«មិនអីទេកូនរបស់ពុក! (ប្រាប់ទៅអ្នកបម្រើ) ចូរយកអាវល្អបំផុតមកបំពាក់ឱ្យវា ហើយយកចិញ្ចៀននឹងស្បែកជើងមកពាក់ឱ្យផង!»

"Men-ei-te koun ro-bos puk! (Broab tov neak-bo-rer) Chour yok arv l-or bom-phot mok bom-peak oy vea, heuy yok chin-chien neng sbek-cherng mok peak oy phong!"

Wing (Father) Wing / [Beaming with pure joy, facing audience/cast]

"Let us have a feast and celebrate! For my son was dead and is alive again; he was lost and is found!"

「我們來吃喝慶祝吧！因為我這個兒子是死而復生，失而復得的！」

«យើងនឹងបរិភោគឱ្យសប្បាយចិត្ត ដ្បិតកូនពុកនេះបានស្លាប់ ឥឡូវរស់ឡើងវិញ បាត់បង់ទៅ ឥឡូវរកឃើញវិញហើយ!»

"Yerng neng bo-ri-phek oy sob-bay-chit, dbit koun puk nih ban slab el-lov roh lerng vinh, bat-bong tov, el-lov rok khern vinh heuy!"

Grace (Narrator) Grace

Now the elder son, Issac, came back from the fields. As he came near the house, he heard the music and dancing.

那時，大兒子阿義（Issac）正在田裡工作。當他回來走近家門，聽見了歡樂的音樂與跳舞聲。

ឯកូនច្បង អាយហ្សេក នៅឯចំការ។ កាលវាត្រឡប់មកជិតដល់ផ្ទះ ក៏ឮសូរភ្លេង និងរបាំយ៉ាងសប្បាយ។

Ek koun-chbong, Issac, noat ek chom-kar. Kal vea tror-lop mok chit dol ptheah, kor leu sour phleng neng ro-bam yeang sob-bay.

Issac (Elder Son) Issac / [To a Bystander / Servant, looking confused]

"What is going on? Why are they celebrating?"

「發生了什麼事？為什麼他們在慶祝？」

«តើគេធ្វើអ្វីហ្នឹង? ហេតុអ្វីបានជាមានការជប់លៀង?»

"Tae kei thver avei neng? Het-avei ban-cheor mean kar chob-leang?"

Bystander (Servant/Friend) Bystander / [Excitedly]

"Your brother has returned! Your father killed the fattened calf because he is home safe and sound!"

「你弟弟安全回來了！你父親因為他平安無恙地回來，特別宰了那隻肥牛犢！」

«ប្អូនលោកត្រឡប់មកវិញហើយ! ហើយលោកឪពុកបានសម្លាប់កូនគោដែលបំប៉ន ដោយព្រោះបានវាមកវិញដោយសុខសាន្ត!»

"Poun lok tror-lop mok vinh heuy! Heuy lok ov-puk ban som-lab koun-ko kdel bom-pon, doy proh ban vea mok vinh doy sok-sant!"

Issac (Elder Son) Issac / [Angry, refusing to enter the house, confronting Father]

"This is not fair! I have worked like a slave for you for years, and you never gave me even a young goat! But for this son of yours who wasted your money, you kill the fatted calf!"

「這不公平！我伺候你這麼多年，你從來沒有給我一隻小山羊讓我慶祝！但他吞盡了你的財產，他一回來，你倒為他宰了肥牛犢！」
